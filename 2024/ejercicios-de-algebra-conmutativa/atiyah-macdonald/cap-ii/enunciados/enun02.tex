Let $A$ be a ring, $\frak a$ an ideal and $M$ an $A$-module. Show that
$(A/\frak a)\tensor[A] M$ is isomorphic to $M/\frak a\,M$.%
\hint{
	Tensor the exact sequence
	\begin{center}
		\begin{tikzcd}[ampersand replacement=\&]
			0\arrow[r] \& {\frak a}\arrow[r] \& A\arrow[r] \&
				A/{\frak a}\arrow[r] \& 0
		\end{tikzcd}
	\end{center}
	with $M$.
}
