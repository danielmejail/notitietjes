Let $A$ be a ring, $\nilrad$ its nilradical. Show that the following are
equivalent:
\begin{enumerate}[(i)]
	\item\label{item:ejer:capi:10:i}
		$A$ has exactly one prime ideal;
	\item\label{item:ejer:capi:10:ii}
		every element of $A$ is either a unit or nilpotent;
	\item\label{item:ejer:capi:10:iii}
		$A/\nilrad$ is a field.
\end{enumerate}
